Welcome to TerraSense

What is TerraSense?

TerraSense is a desktop application that provides a web page allowing you to check and obtain relevant information about the weather at a location. You can view the climatic data for your specific location, as well as get a list of recommended crops to plant.

Climatic Variability and Crop Selection

Farmers face constant challenges due to climatic variability, including unpredictable changes in weather conditions and rainfall patterns. This variability can significantly impact crop yield and the profitability of agricultural operations. Farmers often struggle to make informed decisions about which crops to plant in their fields, especially when the weather is uncertain and climatic patterns are constantly changing. Planting the wrong crop in a season can lead to significant economic losses and negatively affect food security.

What would be the impact on Bolivian agriculture of implementing a crop selection system in response to climatic variability?

Adopting a crop selection system in Bolivia can strengthen the resilience of the agricultural sector to long-term impacts, considering changing climatic patterns and the choice of adapted crops. Locally, a preference for more resistant crops would improve productivity and stability, reducing farmers' vulnerability to extreme weather events. At the regional level, this strategy would influence agricultural diversification and the geographical distribution of crops, optimizing production and mitigating risks associated with climatic variability.
