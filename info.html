More About Us

Who Are We?

TerraSense is a company dedicated to the research and analysis of human activity in relation to the environment. Our goal is to provide reliable and up-to-date information so that users can make informed decisions about their surroundings.

TerraSense Objectives

Our main objective is to develop a Crop Selector Application as part of the technological advancement in the agricultural sector, to respond to the demands from farmers who require qualified tools capable of making informed and strategic decisions in crop selection, taking into account climatic variability in their regions. Through an application that generates a contrast system of crop quality for those responsible for choosing crops.

Recommendations for Using Our Software

  1. Have Experience in the Field of Agriculture
  2. Have Access to the Internet
  3. If you encounter any issues with the application, contact the support team
  4. Consider the history of successful crops in your area
  5. Use Mobile Platform on Desktop Interface for a better experience

Benefits

    User-Friendly Interface

  1. TerraSense has a user-friendly interface that allows users to interpret the results more easily.

    2. Agricultural Productivity

  2. TerraSense helps farmers select crops that are most suitable for their climatic regions. This can help improve crop yield and increase farmers' income.

    3. Reduces Agricultural Risks

  3. TerraSense helps farmers understand factors that can affect crop performance. This can help farmers make informed decisions to reduce agricultural risks.
